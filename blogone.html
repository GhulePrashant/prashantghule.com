Laughter

One of the best feelings in the world which cheer to life is laughter. I will say it is one of the best medicines in the world. Whether it's a smile or a giggle, laughter changes the mood and atmosphere of the surroundings. It makes us feel good. It gives us positive vibes.

Laughter acts as a powerful antidote for pain, stress. I think there is nothing that works faster in order to bring back mind and body into balance than a good laugh. Also, laughter helps us to lighten our burden, connects with the others and keep us focused.

Laughter has so much power to renew and heal one's body and mind. The ability to laugh frequently is the best way to surmount the problems. It supports our emotional health. Laughter increases the blood in your body and also the functioning of blood vessels. Thus, it can help in protecting a person against a heart attack.

Additionally, laughter helps you feel good. The good feeling that you actually get during laughter remains with you even when you stop laughing. Thus, laughter helps you with a positive view in difficult times. A smile or a laugh can do a world of good for you.

Benefits of laughter :

  • Reduce stress

  • Lowers blood pressure

  • Improves cardiovascular health
